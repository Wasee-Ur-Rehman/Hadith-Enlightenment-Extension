About Hadith Enlightenment

Welcome to the Hadith Enlightenment! This extension provides you with a random Hadith from various authentic collections every time you click the extension icon. Our goal is to make it easier for you to access and reflect on the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Features

  • Randomly displays Hadith from multiple authentic sources.
  • Easy-to-use interface with a single click to generate a new Hadith.
  • Light and dark theme options to suit your preference.

Motivation

I developed this extension to help people stay connected with the teachings of Islam in a convenient and engaging way. Whether you are seeking daily inspiration, studying Hadith, or just curious, this tool is designed to be a valuable resource.
